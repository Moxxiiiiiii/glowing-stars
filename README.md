# Glowing Stars

[Warning: Contains NSFW content, minimum age required for play is 18+]

Glowing Stars is an immersive, player-character focused text-based RPG set far into the distant future where Humanity and life alike in the galaxy has colonised large swathes of the stars themselves.
This world will allow you to create your own character, and start a new life on Earth - a wealthy core world ripe with opportunities to make your name, contest with dangerous outlaws and bed many men and women in the process!

Still under development, the game's main planned features are:
  - In depth character creator, with a world responsive to your choices
  - A functional inventory and gear system to complement combat and stat choices allowing for varied builds
  - 10 Locations to explore on the starting world, with other hubs and planets planned for the future.
  - A narrative through-line, complete with main and side-objectives.
  - A variety of lewd content and encounters, embedded with the story.

This is a personal development project, and yet to have an official release, all updates and progress will be visible within the commits, and early access builds will be uploaded upon completion of more core features.
